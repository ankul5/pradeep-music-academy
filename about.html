Pradeep Ramchandra Paanch

At 72 years of age, Pradeep Sir is not just a teacher, but a living legend in Nagpur's musical landscape. With over 50 years of professional experience, his journey is a testament to the timeless power of dedication and passion for the strings.

His teaching legacy spans across the heart of the city. He spent 35 impactful years nurturing talent at his renowned hub in Bajaj Nagar, becoming a foundational figure for thousands of budding musicians. For the last 15 years, he has continued this mission from his premier institute in Gopal Nagar, adapting his deep wisdom to inspire a whole new generation of artists.

As a self-taught master, he possesses a unique understanding of the hurdles a student faces. His philosophy is simple: music is a soul-deep vibration that should bring peace and joy. His patient, structured, and practical approach ensures that every student doesn't just learn notes, but masters the soul of the instrument.

Our Musical Home

This is the iconic class in Gopal Nagar, serving the musical community for over 15 years.

Before moving to this prime location, the institute flourished in Bajaj Nagar for 35 remarkable years. Over the decades, thousands of students have crossed these thresholds, transforming from beginners into accomplished musicians under the guidance of Pradeep Sir.

Every corner of this space resonates with the chords and melodies of five decades of teaching.

Our Journey

1974

The journey began with a single guitar and a passion for self-learning, mastering the strings through sheer dedication.

1989

Launched the first professional coaching sessions in Nagpur, sharing the gift of music with eager learners.

2010

Consolidated all training hubs into a premier musical institution dedicated to Guitar and Keyboard mastery.

Today

Continuing to nurture the next generation of musicians with 50+ years of wisdom and unyielding passion.
